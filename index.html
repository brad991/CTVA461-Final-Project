I am a Videographer and Editor locally based in California.
Filming and editing were my gateway to the creative industry which led to my first steps in the digital world.

Working everyday with several brand and institutions, primarily VitalMX, I pursue my passions with excitement to not only grow my experience and skills but to grow my viewers outlook as well. Persoally, I pursue work in the Worlds of Sports. I have been around the action sports world my entire life mostly within the Motocross Community.

I have always thought that any piece of work I do must trigger an emotion in the viewer, regardless if it is a still or a video. In the process of building this emotion, the experience of traveling the world, creating new relationships with members of society, are all important aspects that help create the same emotion the viewers will experience after watching my projects.

I do think that the combination of social and digital media has enabled me to reach to wide audiences and to get people’s feedback in real-time. This provides me with fresh new creative input to produce new emotional material.
